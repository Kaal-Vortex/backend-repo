In a Tripjack Hotel API response, the fc (Fare Component) object breaks down the hotel rate into base costs, taxes, and service fees. Since this is for a hotel, these values represent the pricing for the selected room and stay duration.
Here is the breakdown of each value from your response:

* BF (Base Fare): The core room rate for the stay (538.82).
* NF (Net Fare): The final amount the agent pays to Tripjack (538.82).
* TF (Total Fare): The gross price shown to the customer (538.82).
* SNP (Supplier Net Price): The raw cost provided by the hotel or supplier (495.7).
* SBP (Seller Base Price): The base price defined by the seller/aggregator before fees (512.49).
* SGP (Seller Gross Price): The total price calculated for the seller including their margins (538.82).
* TMF (Total Markup Fare): The total markup added by the agency or system (43.12).
* TSF (Total Service Fee): The standard service fee applied to the booking (26.33).
* TTSF (Total Transaction Service Fee): A specific fee for processing the transaction (26.33).
* SAC (Service apical charge): An additional administrative or service charge (43.12).
* CMU (Commission): The commission earned by the agent on this specific hotel booking (0.0).
* SRC (Service Reward/Charge): Any extra rewards or specific service adjustments (0.0).

In the MakeMyTrip (MMT) API, the priceDetail object breaks down the hotel room cost to show the customer exactly what they are paying versus the original "rack rate."
Here is what each value in your response represents:

* price: The original "strike-through" or rack rate before any discounts (6,249).
* displayPrice / discountedPrice: The base rate shown to the user after MMT or hotel discounts are applied, but before taxes (4,999).
* priceWithTax: What the original price (6,249) would have been if you added taxes to it (6,499).
* discountedPriceWithTax: The discounted base rate (4,999) plus the tax (5,249).
* discountedPriceWithTaxAndFees: The final "all-in" price the customer pays (5,249). Since totalAdditionalFees is 0, this matches the price with tax.
* totalTax: The specific GST/VAT amount applied to the discounted price (250).
* totalTaxWithFees: The sum of taxes and any extra service fees (250).
* totalAdditionalFees: Any extra charges like resort fees or convenience fees (0).
* pricingKey: An internal identifier used by MMT to track the specific pricing logic used ("DEFAULT").
* ratePlanCode: The unique ID for the specific room deal/package (e.g., Room Only vs. Room with Breakfast).
